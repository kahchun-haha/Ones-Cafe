Signature Beef Burger

A marvel of gastronomic flair, this burger is crafted with our exclusive chili coriander beef patty that promises a burst of flavors with each mouthful. Tucked within a soft, sesame-studded bun are layers of succulent beef bacon, crisp tomatoes, tangy gherkins, and velvety gruyere cheese. A spread of rich guacamole and a side of golden fries complete this masterpiece. It’s not just a burger; it's a journey of taste that defines the essence of our café.

customer's reviews

what they say

Sunita Gandhi

Every visit to Ones Café is a delight. The Smoked Salmon Sandwich, with its perfectly poached egg and caper mayo, is sublime. However, I do wish they would expand their dessert menu; the choices are good but a bit predictable. It's also a shame that their Sundae often runs out by the evening!

Rohit Thakur

Love the ambiance at Ones Café and the Tenderloin Steak is absolutely delectable — cooked to your liking and perfectly seasoned. On the downside, service can slow down during busy times, so plan accordingly. But if you're not in a hurry, the quality of the meals makes the wait worthwhile.

S.S Mishra

For a unique treat, try the Ones Cafe signature beef burger — it’s rich and packed with unique toppings like chili coriander and beef bacon. But note, the cafe can feel a bit cramped and gets noisy, which might not suit those looking for a quiet evening out.

Rajeev Chaterjee

Ones Café's Spaghetti Carbonara is creamy and delicious, but a little less garlic would make it perfect. Also, I noticed that sometimes the music is just a bit too loud, making it hard to have a conversation. The staff is friendly though, and they make up for any little shortcomings with their warm service.

Mohmd. Ali Shah

Ones Café never misses with their Teriyaki Chicken Pizza — it’s a unique twist that keeps me coming back. However, their online reservation system could use some tweaking. I’ve had a few glitches while trying to book a table. In person, the experience is seamless, but they could enhance their digital interface.

Monica Oberoi

The Spinach & Cream Cheese Pizza is a revelation for vegetarians at Ones Café! It’s creamy, rich, and just divine. But I do think their plating for desserts like the Sizzling Brownies could be more imaginative. It’s delicious, but the presentation doesn’t always match the taste. Still, it’s a solid choice for a relaxed meal.
